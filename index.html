Welcome to OCEANDRO shipyard

OCEANDRO Large Yacht Builders is a leading shipyard expert in new building & repairing business in the Middle East area. The shipyard had been established in the free zone of Suez in Egypt ten years ago.

What we do?

We offer the full satisfaction to our customer as we have up to date standards & regulations with systems education, documentation and state of the art shipboard computer monitoring. Certified for welding of hull structure by DNV.GL. Our experienced technicians provide the utmost of technical assistance. Our client commitment program reaches all ports of call worldwide ... anywhere, anytime the shipyard is equipped with:

  • 900 tons lifting capacity mechanical slipway.
  • 3000 Tons Capacity Dry Dock.
  • Service Jetty is 150 lengths for the repair work & ships during a float.
  • Entrance cannel to the shipyard having 5.0M Depth/Draft in low tide & 7.0 M Depth/Draft in high tide
  • The waiting area nearby the shipyard can accommodate ships up to 5 m draft.
